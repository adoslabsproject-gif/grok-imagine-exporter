# Privacy Policy — Grok Imagine Exporter

_Last updated: 2026_

Grok Imagine Exporter is a browser extension that runs **entirely on your device**.

## What it does
- It lists and downloads **your own** images and videos generated on Grok Imagine, using your **already-active grok.com session**.
- All requests go **only** to Grok/xAI endpoints you are already authenticated with (`grok.com`, `assets.grok.com`, `imagine-public.x.ai`).

## Data collection
- The extension does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.
- It does **not** send any data to the developer or to any third-party server.
- There is **no analytics, tracking, or telemetry**.

## Local storage
- The only data stored is a local list of asset IDs you have already downloaded (kept in the browser's `storage`), used to skip duplicates on subsequent runs.
- This list never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time from the extension.

## Permissions
- `downloads` — to save files to your Downloads folder.
- `storage` — to remember already-downloaded items (local only).
- Host access to `grok.com`, `assets.grok.com`, `imagine-public.x.ai` — to read and download your own content.
